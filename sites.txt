🇬🇧 UK

yell.com — The UK's main yellow pages equivalent. It has over 2.9 million registered companies and more than 3,000 business classifications. PopArt Studio Contains name, phone, address, and often email.
thomsonlocal.com — Another strong UK directory with local maps and contact info.


🇺🇸 USA

yellowpages.com — The direct US equivalent of gelbeseiten.de. Listings contain company names, phone numbers, addresses, ratings, and service categories. Web Scraper Emails are available on individual business profile pages.
yelp.com — Also widely used in the US, with business name, phone, address, and sometimes email.


🇩🇰 Denmark

krak.dk — The main Danish business directory. Krak.dk is operated by Eniro Group, which has about 5.2 million unique visitors per week across its Nordic platforms. Wikipedia
degulesider.dk — Also operated by Eniro, a complementary Danish yellow pages site.


🇳🇴 Norway

gulesider.no — Norway's primary yellow pages. It's Norway's exclusive yellow pages directory, visited by over 1.5 million users per week. Yext Contains name, phone, address, and website.


🇦🇹 Austria

herold.at — Herold.at is listed as a supported Austrian business directory alongside local.ch (Switzerland) and gelbeseiten.de (Germany) Ypspider, making it the most direct equivalent for Austria.
